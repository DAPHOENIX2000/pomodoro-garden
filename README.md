# 🌱 Pomodoro Garden

A pixel-art Pomodoro timer where focus sessions grow a garden. Quit early and a plant wilts. Fully 8-bit styled with animated clouds, a pixel sun, and a living garden grid.

## ✨ Features
- **Pixel art scene** — Animated clouds, spinning pixel sun, grass, trees, and fence
- **3 timer modes** — 🍅 Focus (25m), ☕ Short Break (5m), 🌿 Long Break (15m)
- **Garden grid** — 24 plots that fill with random plants as you complete sessions
- **Plant variety** — 15 different plant emojis that appear as you progress
- **Wilt mechanic** — Reset mid-session and a plant wilts with a pixel animation
- **Plant unlocks** — Reach session milestones to unlock new plant types
- **Stats tracking** — Total sessions, minutes focused, day streak
- **Day streak** — Tracks consecutive study days
- **Pixel toast notifications** — Retro-styled notifications on completion

## 🎯 How to Use
1. Click **▶ START** to begin a focus session
2. Work until the timer hits 0 — a plant will grow!
3. Take a break, then start another session
4. Don't reset mid-session or your last plant wilts 🥀
5. Fill all 24 plots to complete your garden
